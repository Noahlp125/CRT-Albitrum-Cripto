## 🦊 How to Add the Token to MetaMask

To see this token in your wallet, follow these steps:

1. Open **MetaMask** and ensure you are on the **Arbitrum One** network.
2. Scroll down to the bottom of the "Tokens" tab and click **"Import Tokens"**.
3. Paste the following address into the **"Token Contract Address"** field:
   `0xe0D8a3207aD560578032886CB14155ECbb864685`
4. Click **"Add Custom Token"** and then **"Import Tokens"**.
